How We Work

From discovery call
to a running system.

No discovery that leads nowhere. No proposal that doesn't match delivery. Here's exactly how an engagement moves — and what you can count on at every step.

Start with a Call
The Engagement

Five stages, six weeks,
one system that's yours.

01
Discovery & Strategy Call
20 minutes — no pitch

We map your ICP, your current stack, and where revenue leaks. You leave knowing what a real system looks like for your business — and whether we're the right team to build it.

02
System Blueprint
Within 48 hours

A written proposal: which layers we build, in what order, on what timeline, and what each will do. Every tool and account created in your name. You approve before anything moves.

03
Build
Weeks 1–4

We engineer the system — positioning, sequences, AI agents, CRM, automations, dashboards. First pipeline starts moving inside two weeks, with a strategy call every week.

04
Launch & Optimize
Weeks 4–6

The full system goes live. We read the data and tune it — targeting, messaging, qualification, conversion — until it's at full velocity. Nothing set-and-forget.

05
Ownership
Yours to keep

Every account, domain, and asset is registered in your business name, documented, and handed over with training. Keep running it yourself, or keep us on to optimize.
