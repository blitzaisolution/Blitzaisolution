How We Work

From discovery call
to a running system.

No endless discovery. No proposal that doesn't match what shows up. Here's exactly how it moves, and what you can count on at every step.

Start with a Call
The Engagement

Five stages, six weeks,
one system that's yours.

01
Discovery & Strategy Call
20 minutes, no pitch

We look at how you sell today, what tools you use, and where customers slip away. You leave knowing what a real system would do for your business, and whether we're the right team to build it.

02
System Blueprint
Within 48 hours

A written proposal: which layers we build, in what order, on what timeline, and what each will do. Every tool and account created in your name. You approve before anything moves.

03
Build
Weeks 1 to 4

We build the pieces. Positioning, sequences, AI agents, CRM, automations, dashboards. First meetings usually start landing inside two weeks, and we meet every week to keep it moving.

04
Launch & Optimize
Weeks 4 to 6

The full system goes live. We watch the numbers and improve what's working, targeting, messaging, timing, until it's running at full speed. Nothing gets set and forgotten.

05
Ownership
Yours to keep

Every account, domain, and asset is registered in your business name, documented, and handed over with training. Keep running it yourself, or keep us on to optimize.
