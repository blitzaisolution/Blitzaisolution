Step by Step

Six weeks from first call
to full system velocity.

No endless discovery. No SOW that takes three weeks to write. We move fast, communicate weekly, and hand over a system that's already running.

Week 0
Strategy Call
20 minutes — no pitch, no upsell

We look at your current setup, your ICP, and where leads are slipping. You'll walk away knowing exactly what a real system looks like for your business — and whether we're the right team to build it.

  • Current tech stack and process review
  • ICP and offer clarity check
  • Honest gap assessment — no soft-pedaling
Week 1
Proposal + Scope
Delivered within 48 hours

A written proposal with an itemized build scope, week-by-week timeline, and the exact investment. No surprises. You approve before we touch anything.

  • Itemized deliverables — no vague line items
  • Every tool, domain, and account named upfront
  • All registered in your business name from day one
Weeks 1–2
Foundation Setup
Infrastructure before anything goes live

GHL sub-account configured, domains purchased and warming, inboxes set up, integrations wired. CRM pipeline architecture built to match how you actually sell. First leads begin flowing by end of week two.

  • GHL sub-account setup under your business name
  • Sending domains purchased and warm-up protocols started
  • Pipeline architecture, stages, and lead scoring configured
Weeks 2–4
System Build
All channels, all layers

AI agents trained on your offer and objections. Outbound sequences written and live. Inbound ads running with AI qualification on every lead. Nurture drips triggered by behavior. Weekly sync every Friday without exception.

  • AI SDR agents trained and tested in your accounts
  • Cold email, LinkedIn, and SMS sequences live
  • Meta ads running with full lead capture and routing
  • Nurture drips active for every ICP segment
  • Weekly Friday sync — data, decisions, next steps
Weeks 4–6
Iteration + Optimization
Data drives every change

Full system live. We review real performance data and iterate — sequence open rates, ad efficiency, AI qualification accuracy, reply-to-booking rates. Every change tied to a number that moved.

  • Full attribution tracking by channel and source
  • A/B testing across sequences and ad creative
  • Performance dashboard live and shared with your team
Day 90+
Full Ownership Transfer
Everything yours — it always was

Every account, domain, contact list, sequence, and automation handed over with full documentation and team training. The system runs without us. Ongoing retainer available if you want us to stay in.

  • Complete asset and account transfer
  • Team training sessions on day-to-day operations
  • SOPs and documentation in your format of choice
  • Optional ongoing optimization retainer
How We Operate

What you can count on,
every engagement.

01

Weekly calls. No exceptions.

Every Friday, you get a sync. Data reviewed, decisions made, next week planned. Not a status update — an operating meeting. If we're not in it, we're not building it right.

02

Built in your accounts.

Every tool, domain, and sequence is registered in your business name from day one. When the engagement ends, you walk away with everything. No proprietary platform, no lock-in.

03

We scope before we build.

You see the full scope — itemized, priced, timed — before we start. No surprises at invoice. No scope changes without your sign-off. What we propose is what we deliver.

04

We stay until it works.

We don't hand over a system that isn't running. If the first sequence underperforms, we rewrite it. If the AI agent needs another training pass, we do it. Full velocity before handover.

05

Everything is documented.

Every workflow, every prompt, every sequence logic is documented in plain language. Your team can run this without us. That's the point.

06

We tell you when it's not working.

If an ad isn't converting or a sequence has a structural problem, we say so. No spin, no optimism theater. You get the data and our honest read on what to do next.
