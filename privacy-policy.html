Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2025 · BlitzAI Solutions LLC

BlitzAI Solutions LLC ("BlitzAI," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to theblitzaisolutions.com and the services we provide.

Information we collect

  • Information you give us, such as your name, email address, phone number, company, and anything you include when you book a call, fill out a form, or email us.
  • Usage information, such as pages visited, referring source, device and browser type, collected through cookies and analytics tools.
  • Communications, records of your correspondence with us, including scheduling and messages.

How we use your information

  • To respond to inquiries, schedule calls, and provide our services.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website and offerings.
  • To send you information you request or that is relevant to a service you engaged us for.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

SMS & messaging consent

If you provide your phone number and opt in, we may send you SMS messages related to your inquiry or engagement (for example, scheduling and follow-ups). Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP, and reply HELP for help. We do not sell or share mobile opt-in data with third parties for their own marketing.

Cookies & analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to run the site and understand how it's used. We may use analytics and advertising services (such as Google Analytics and the Meta Pixel). You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Service providers

We share information with trusted providers that help us operate, for example, our CRM and scheduling platform (GoHighLevel), email and hosting providers, and analytics tools. These providers process data on our behalf and are bound to protect it.

Data retention & security

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described here or as required by law, and we use reasonable safeguards to protect it. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to certain processing. To make a request, email syed@theblitzaisolutions.com.
